Green Card

If you have recently become married to a U.S. citizen, you may be wondering if you can obtain citizenship yourself. First of all - congratulations! Our team at IRC is ready to help you and your spouse complete the journey of love and begin your lives together in the United States!

By obtaining a Green Card, the spouse of a current U.S. citizen can both live and work in any part of the United States of America. Once an individual obtains their Green Card, they will hold a “permanent resident” identification until the day they wish to apply for naturalization as a full U.S. citizen. The citizenship process can begin after three years.

For many individuals, obtaining a Green Card is the natural way to start the journey of becoming a citizen. However, the process can be stressful and complicated. At IRC, our team of skilled immigration professionals can help you walk through the process from the initial filling of your application, to the finishing line of your process.

The following are the different types of Green Card applications.

  • Adjustment Status (green card)
  • Green Card Replacement
  • Green Card Lottery
  • VAWA : Violence Against Women Act

Naturalization Applications

Are you ready to become a naturalized U.S. citizen? The process may seem overwhelming and intimidating, but with the right help by your side, you can quickly and successfully complete your journey to full citizenship.

In order to begin the naturalization process, you must complete the Application for Naturalization form - also known as Form N-400. This application begins the process for current green card holders to apply for full citizenship through the U.S. government. Before you begin - there are many eligibility requirements that must be met. IRC’s team of trained immigration advisors stand ready to make sure that you have met all the requirements to be considered for naturalization.

From filling out the N-400 with the U.S. Citizenship and Immigration Services (USCIS) Department to completing the steps to fully comply with the expectations of the Department of Homeland Security, IRC is ready to guide you step by step through the naturalization process.

At an affordable cost, our team can help you complete your citizenship application, collect all necessary and required documents and walk you step by step and hand in hand, we can help you make your citizenship dreams come true. Your American dream starts today!

The following are the application processes we can help you with.

  • Naturalization Adult
  • Naturalization child applying under his/her parent
  • Child Birth Abroad (Package)
  • Medical Certification Disability Exemptions

Visa Applications

For many countries, the ability to quickly and effortlessly travel across international borders requires a current and valid visa. While the requirement of having a visa may seem simple, the process of obtaining one can be complicated and covered in red tape.

If you are beginning the process of applying for a visa, the team at IRCO stands ready to help you each step of the way. From applications to processing, our team of skilled immigration experts can give you peace of mind while you prepare to travel.
